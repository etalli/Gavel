Built a physical gavel for Claude Code.

Three buttons: Allow Once, Always Allow, Reject. LEDs notify you of Claude's state — so you always know when a ruling is needed. Any keyboard could send the same keys — but a dedicated device makes you stop and think. A ruling, not a reflex.
